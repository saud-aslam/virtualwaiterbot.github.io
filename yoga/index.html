How Does it Work

Placing Order from Virtual Waiter Bot

  • Scan the QR code on your table to open the Virtual Waiter Bot.
  • Browse the menu, select and customize items, then add them to your cart.
  • Proceed to checkout, enter your phone and table number, and submit your order.
  • Your order is sent to the kitchen and will be served to your table.

Receiving Order from Virtual Waiter Bot

  • Kitchen receives the order details.
  • Customer is informed when the order is ready for pickup and collects it from the counter.
